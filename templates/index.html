Instructions:

Objective: Build and bake pizzas to score points.

How to Play Round 1:

  • Prepare ingredients by clicking the buttons (Base, Sauce, Ham, Pineapple). This ingredients pool is shared by the whole room.
  • Drag and drop ingredients into your Pizza Builder to assemble your pizza.
  • If you are on mobile or tablet, just tap to select an ingredient and then tap the Pizza Builder to "drop" it in that area.
  • In this round you only have access to your own pizza builder area.
  • A valid pizza requires:
    • 1 base
    • 1 sauce
    • Either 4 ham (for a bacon pizza) or 2 ham + 2 pineapple (for a pineapple pizza).
  • Submit your pizza once built. Valid pizzas will display with an emoji overlay:
    • üçï + ü•ì for bacon pizzas
    • üçï + üçç for pineapple pizzas
  • Invalid pizzas show a üçï with a üö´ overlay.
  • Move pizzas to the oven to bake‚Äîthey will continue to be shown as emoji.
  • After baking, pizzas are evaluated:
    • Undercooked (<30 seconds) pizzas get a yellow tint.
    • Burnt (>45 seconds) pizzas are tinted dark.

How to Play Round 2:

  • Drag or tap ingredients from the Shared Ingredients pool to any of the Shared Pizza Builders.
  • Any player can add ingredients to any shared builder and submit pizzas from them.
  • Collaboration is key‚Äîwork together to optimize pizza production!

How to Play Round 3:

  • Use the Shared Pizza Builders to collaborate, as in Round 2.
  • 50 customer orders arrive throughout the round, specifying exact ingredient combinations:
    • Ham: 1 base, 1 sauce, 4 ham
    • Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 4 pineapple
    • Ham & Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 2 ham, 2 pineapple
    • Light Ham: 1 base, 1 sauce, 1 ham
    • Light Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 1 pineapple
    • Plain: 1 base, 1 sauce
    • Heavy Ham: 1 base, 1 sauce, 6 ham
    • Heavy Pineapple: 1 base, 1 sauce, 6 pineapple
  • Build pizzas to match these orders exactly. Unmatched pizzas are wasted.
  • Orders arrive over time, with the last one at least 45 seconds before the round ends.
